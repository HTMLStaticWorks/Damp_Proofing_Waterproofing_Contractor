Structural & Basement Waterproofing

Type A, B, and C waterproofing compliance with BS 8102:2022 codes.

BS 8102:2022 Structural Standards

Waterproofing structures below ground level requires specialist design to withstand hydrostatic water pressure. The British Standard BS 8102:2022 defines three distinct waterproofing types to keep earth-retaining walls dry.

HydroShield specializes in custom Type A and Type C waterproofing applications. These protect crawl spaces, commercial cellars, and luxury basement conversions from groundwater leaks.

Three Forms of Protection

Type A (Barrier Protection):
Applying multi-layer cement slurry coats or bitumen sheets to outer or inner wall surfaces.
Type B (Integrity Protection):
Designing water-resistant reinforced concrete structures (with waterstops).
Type C (Cavity Drainage):
Installing internal studded membranes and perimeter drain channels to direct leaks to a sump pump.

Technical Setup

Type C Cavity Drainage Specifications

Instead of trying to hold back hydrostatic water pressure, Type C cavity drainage systems safely capture and divert leaks to a sump pump.

The Cavity Drainage Anatomy

We install high-density polyethylene studded membranes along internal brick walls and basement floors. This forms a clear cavity wall gap that allows water to seep down into a perimeter drainage channel (recessed below slab level).

This channel directs moisture to a dual-pump sump station. The walls are finished with standard metal studs and moisture-resistant plasterboard, allowing for standard paint coats and high-quality room finishes while keeping concrete foundations completely dry.

Maintenance System

Sump Pump Care & Wireless Telemetry

A basement drainage system is only as reliable as its sump pump. We ensure 24/7 reliability through telemetry monitoring.

Dual-Pump Backup

We mount primary and secondary pumps in the same station. If the main pump encounters blocks or fails, the backup pump activates automatically.

Battery Backups

Power grid failures are common during severe storms. Our systems feature a dedicated power battery backup capable of clearing up to 10,000 liters of water without mains power.

Wireless Alarms

Installing intelligent telemetry sensors that send high-water alarms directly to your mobile phone, highlighting blocks or issues before they can cause damage.
