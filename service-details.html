Technical Specification

Timber Treatment & Preservation

Engineering safeguards against dry rot, wet rot, and woodworm insect infestations.

Wood Decay Protection Workflow

Wood rot happens when wood-destroying fungi attack building timber under high humidity profiles. Dry rot (Serpula lacrymans) is especially dangerous as it can grow through brickwork to infect nearby floors.

Preservation Methodology

1

Source Removal

We trace and fix the moisture source first (e.g. leaking roofs, subfloor condensation, masonry damp courses).

2

Damaged Timber Removal

Infected timbers are cut back past the visible fungal growth zone and replaced with pre-treated timber joists.

3

Chemical Barrier Injections

Surrounding brickwork masonry is treated with premium fungicidal washes to halt further microscopic spore movement.
