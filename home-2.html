Diagnostics Flow

Scientific Moisture Diagnosis

How we analyze structural damp issues before prescribing waterproofing solutions.

STAGE 01

Thermal Profiling

We map interior wall temperatures using micro-bolometer cameras to isolate condensation cold spots.

STAGE 02

Electrical Conductivity

Pinless and pin moisture meters determine the exact relative humidity of concrete and brick structures.

STAGE 03

Hygroscopic Analysis

Salt analyses distinguish between groundwater rising damp salts and simple surface condensation problems.
