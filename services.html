Why HydroShield Standards Lead the Industry

We believe in diagnostics-first waterproofing. Our surveying methods comply strictly with British Standard BS 8102:2022 and PCA codes of practice. Every surveyor carries digital micro-bolometer thermal cameras, concrete density meters, and calcium carbide test kits to isolate hygroscopic ground salts from simple surface condensation.

PCA Code Compliant

Full certification audits on every structural timber and basement membrane install.

Salt Profiling

Laboratory chloride and nitrate tests to ensure absolute accuracy in our reports.
