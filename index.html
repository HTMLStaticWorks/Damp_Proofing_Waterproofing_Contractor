Diagnostics & Analysis

Common Damp Problems

Moisture ingress ruins buildings from the inside out. Identifying symptoms early saves thousands in structural renovations.

Rising Damp

Groundwater travelling upwards through masonry structures via capillary action, leaving hygroscopic salts and structural damage.

Learn Treatment

Penetrating Damp

External moisture penetrating through structural cracks, defective bricks, or faulty roofing systems causing internal wet rot.

Learn Treatment

Condensation & Mold

Excess humidity on cold interior surfaces creating the perfect environment for toxic black mold outbreaks and respiratory risks.

Learn Treatment
Property Security

Property Risk Assessment

Uncontrolled moisture degrades structural integrity and devalues your home. Discover the risks and protect your asset.

Foundation Decay

Continuous contact with groundwater softens brickwork mortar joints, threatening the building load capacity.

Timber Rot

Damp floors and joists encourage spores that cause dry rot, destroying support structural beams in timber.

Health Hazards

Toxic spores trigger or exacerbate asthma, bronchitis, and common respiratory allergies in residents.
