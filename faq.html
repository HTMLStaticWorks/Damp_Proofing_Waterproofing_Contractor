Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about structural moisture diagnostics and property warranties.

What happens during a technical damp survey?

Our surveyor will inspect the affected walls inside and out using specialized electrical damp meters and thermal cameras. They will audit the damp proof courses, external brick ground levels, subfloor ventilation slots, and gutter flows, followed by a formal written specification report.

How long does a DPC chemical injection take to dry?

While the silicone cream forms an instant barrier, the moisture trapped in the brickwork above the DPC must evaporate. As a rule of thumb, walls dry out at a rate of 1 month per 25mm of wall thickness (e.g. up to 9 months for solid 9-inch walls).

What warranties do you provide on waterproofing systems?

All physical damp course installations, chemical creams injections, and cavity basement tanking systems carry a 25-year structural guarantee, which is transferable to new homeowners if you sell the property.
